## How to Read Papers Effectively

1. **Read the abstract and conclusion first** to understand the main contribution
2. **Look at figures and tables** — they often tell the story faster than text
3. **Read related work** to understand context
4. **Focus on the method section** for implementation details
5. **Check the appendix** for hyperparameters and ablations
6. **Use tools**: [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/), [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/), [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/)
